## About {#about}

I am a Ph.D. student in mathematics at Harvard University advised by [Peter Kronheimer](https://people.math.harvard.edu/~kronheim/). Previously, I received my B.S. in mathematics from MIT in 2023, where I was advised by [Tom Mrowka](https://math.mit.edu/directory/profile.html?pid=192).

I am interested in low-dimensional topology, particularly applications of knot theory to the study of 3- and 4-manifolds and categorified link invariants. I am fortunate to be supported by a Harvard Graduate Prize Fellowship, and I am affiliated with a [Simons Collaboration](https://sites.google.com/view/simons-newstructures/home).
