About

I am a Ph.D. student in mathematics at Harvard University advised by Peter Kronheimer. Previously, I received my B.S. in mathematics from MIT in 2023, where I was advised by Tom Mrowka.

I am interested in low-dimensional topology, particularly applications of knot theory to the study of 3- and 4-manifolds and categorified link invariants. I am fortunate to be supported by a Harvard Graduate Prize Fellowship, and I am affiliated with a Simons Collaboration.
