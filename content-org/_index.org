* About
I am a Ph.D. student in mathematics at Harvard University advised by [[https://people.math.harvard.edu/~kronheim/][Peter Kronheimer]]. Previously, I received my B.S. in mathematics from MIT in 2023, where I was advised by [[https://math.mit.edu/directory/profile.html?pid=192][Tom Mrowka]].

I am interested in low-dimensional topology, particularly applications of knot theory to the study of 3- and 4-manifolds and categorified link invariants. I am fortunate to be supported by a Harvard Graduate Prize Fellowship, and I am affiliated with a [[https://sites.google.com/view/simons-newstructures/home][Simons Collaboration]].
